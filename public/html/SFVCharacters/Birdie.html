Overview:

Birdie plays the mid-to-far range well.


Strengths:

Weaknesses:

  • lacks a solid reversal

Special Moves:

Move Input Notes
Bull Head qcf + P EX gains armor
Bull Horn hold p or k then release projectile invulerability, EX gains armor
Hanging Chain qcf + K Chain goes thru projectiles. LK opponent lands close, MK opponent lands far, HK anti airs
Killing Head hcb + P
Bull Revenger hcb + K projectile invulnerability

V Skill: Banana Time, Drink Time, Break Time!

MP + MK: Birdie eats a donut and gains Variable meter.
Back + MP + MK: A stationary banana peel trap. Bit of meter and you can combo out of.
Down + MP + MK: Totally not a Red Bull. Bit of meter, rolls on the floor, does not hit low, also doesn't count as a projectile...

V Trigger: Enjoy Time!

Birdie eats a pepper, which gives him more damage, and buffs his special. Specials become faster, gain armor, and/or hit more times!
