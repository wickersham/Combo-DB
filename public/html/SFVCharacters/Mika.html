V Skill: Mic Preformance

Speech can be held, Mika has one hit of armor and throws her microphone upon release. This not only gains V meter but powers up her throws depending on the level of shit talked.

V Trigger: Nadeshiko

MP + MK: From above Nadeshiko belly flops on top of the opponent. Hold for a delayed hit.
f + MP + HK: From behind Nadeshiko drop kicks the opponent. Hold for a delayed hit. Always block in relation to Mika, not Nadeshiko.
b + MP + MK: From the front Nadeshiko drop kicks. Hold for a delayed hit and always block the direction of Mika.
