Zangief


Overview:

Gief first wants to establish that the opponent is scared of his buttons. That's when his grabs can put the hurt on.


Strengths:

  • big health and stun bar
  • armored moves
  • damage
  • his iron body is invincible, so beware* (body not actually invincible)
  • he is the red cyclone

Weaknesses:

  • lacks a solid reversal
  • Anti air game not the strongest

Special Moves:

Move Input Notes
Screw Pile Driver 360 + P L: most range and fastest, least dmg. H: least range, slowest, most damage
Borscht Dynamite air 360 + P alters trajectory except EX
Lariat PPP projectile invincible, must hit way early if you want to anti air with it
Siberian Express hcb + K EX has hit of armor

V Skill: Iron Muscle

Gives Zangief one hit of armor (unlimited hits in V trigger), attacks upon release. Forward makes him walk forward

V Trigger: Cyclone Lariat

Projectile invinciblity after some startup, vacuums the opponent in if close enough, can extend combos or set up grabs. If the whole thing hits (hold HP + HK) you can follow up with EX Borsht or headbutt.
