Street Fighter V


NAME: c. MK > Hadoken , Shunku Hadoken
damage: 344 AKA: low medium kick, fireball, super

Ah, the tried and true! The bread and butter, the Ryu special, the "you don't even know how to do one cool combo? try this one." Not as safe on block as it was in USFIV but the super part is hit-confirmable. Buffer the fireball if you are fishing with crouch MK and if it whiffs the fireball won't come out! magic! Don't get whiff-punished! And if you see a Ryu sticking his foot out all day fishing for this combo, whiff-punish that sucka!
stun: 190
tags: easy, hit confirm, low start,
